Ryan M's Biking Adventures

Day 2 - Lochsa to Kooskia, ID

Track my route here

The day started out cloudy but was relatively nice. About 20 miles in we started getting rain that lasted about an hour. Once the rain stopped the clouds started clearing out and we ended up having sun for the rest of the day. All in all we couldn't have asked for better weather.

About 40 miles into the ride, Austin's tire sidewall ripped and he ended up falling off his bike. He and the bike were mostly fine. He broke his headlight on the front of the bike, but the rest was fine. It took him about an hour to sew the sidewall. I'm carrying a spare tire, but unfortunately it was the wrong size. He and his girlfriend followed us to Kooskia where they'll stay for a few days until a new tire can be shipped to them. There aren't any bike shops for about 100 miles so it's their only option.

We stopped briefly at Ryan's Wilderness Inn for food. Great name if you ask me.

Tomorrow will be the toughest day so far. Today was almost completely flat. Tomorrow will be a big day of climbing and 105 miles.
