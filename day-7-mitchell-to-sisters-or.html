Day 7 - Mitchell to Sisters, OR

Track my route here

Today might have been one of my best days yet. The hostel I stayed at was fantastic. It was in an old church that they had converted. The hosts were very nice and treated us to great food, showers and comfy beds.

Since I had ridden so far yesterday, I had considered doing a shorter recovery day. I left late at 11am and the day started with a 10 mile climb. What I didn't realize is that the next 25 miles were either downhill or flat. I ended up flying into Prineville by 2pm and figured I might as well keep going. I felt good and the day was early.

The roads between Prineville and Sisters were great. Very few cars (with the exception of the asshole who coal rolled me) and great scenery.

I made it to Sisters by 6pm. I had managed to ride 95 miles in 6 hours, which is really good time, especially with weight.

Tonight I went to a nice restaurant and enjoyed some ravioli and a local porter. I'm going to visit the bike shop in the morning to stock up on some tubes since I'm carrying the wrong size from the day before.
