Ryan M's Biking Adventures

Day 3 - Kooskia to New Meadows, ID

Track my route here

Today was my first solo day of the trip. Everything started out more-or-less fine. There was a really long and steep climb in the first 10 miles. It was tough but it was followed by a great 7 mile descent.

Unfortunately at the bottom of the hill things changed a little. I heard a small sound and didn't really think much of it, but soon realized I had heard that sound before. I looked at my back tire and saw a slight wobble. I had broken a spoke. It couldn't have been in a worse place. There wasn't any kind of bike shop within 120 miles. Fortunately I had my Kevlar thread spoke on me. It took far too long to get it on, but the wheel was true and I managed to ride the remaining 60 miles on the wheel.

I stopped tonight in New Meadows. Still no bike shop, but there is a town 10 miles away, up a very steep hill, that has a couple shops. I'm hoping one of them can get a new spoke on. Fortunately the lady at the front desk at the BnB mentioned that the owner needs to go there tomorrow morning and could probably give me a ride. Hoping that works out. Otherwise I'll be riding at least another 120 miles on the wheel as it is.
