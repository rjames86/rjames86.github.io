Ryan M's Biking Adventures

Day 11 - Port Orford to Klamath, CA

Track my route here

Today was foggy. All day. Fortunately it wasn't too cold.

This picture says it all. I'm back in California! The hardest part now is seeing all the road signs reminding me how many miles I have left. In some crazy fantasy, I keep thinking "well if I ride all day and night, I could get back in less than two days."

I passed a few riders today, but not nearly like yesterday. What baffles me is that I'll wave to passing riders and they hardly acknowledge me. We're all out here riding our bikes and you can't even be excited or courteous enough to see hi to a fellow rider?

Once I hit the redwoods everything got even better. There was a bunch of road construction going over this pass. The traffic guy let me go anyways and so I had the whole pass to myself nearly the entire time. It was amazing. Complete silence.

Today I hit a few milestones. I'm in my final state, and I crossed over 1000 miles in just over 10 days. My average has been around 94-95 miles a day. If things go well, I'll be back in SF in three days.
