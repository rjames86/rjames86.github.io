Ryan M's Biking Adventures

Day 10 - Florence to Port Orford, OR

Track my route here

Today was a nice day to ride. While there was a ridiculous amount of traffic, the shoulders were wide and the pavement was really smooth. It was mostly flat with some gradual climbs but I managed to ride 103 miles in just over 6 hours.

Having the ocean to my right for some of the day was nice. It was never warmer than about 65 degrees and it was always green.

I went through a few nice towns but for the most part, it was nothing but highways. I passed maybe 6-8 cyclists throughout the day. Everyone was doing their own thing so I didn't chat with any of them on the road. I did sit and have coffee with a woman who was biking from Victoria to Mexico. There was a sourdough bakery about 25 miles south of Florence that I couldn't pass up. It was almost 12 and I figured a sourdough bagel wouldn't hurt any.

Tonight I'm staying in Port Orford. I had dinner at The Crazy Norwegian Fish and Chips. The food was amazing and cheap. Tomorrow I'll be back in California!
