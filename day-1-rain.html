Day 1 - Rain

Track my route here

I left a day early since I knew rain was coming and I wouldn't have to ride quite as far. Turned out to be one of my best ideas ever. Within the first 10 minutes of riding the rain started and continued throughout the entire day.

Before riding, I stopped at Adventure Cycles in Missoula. I looked at the visitor book and saw there were a few people who had checked in that morning and were riding the same route I was. I assumed I would see them by the end of the day. I decided to take a break 40 miles in at Lolo Hot Springs and saw three bikes sitting outside. I walked in and they immediately invited me over to eat with them. We rode the rest of the day together.

The photos from today didn't turn out great from all the rain. Most of the photos are blurry from all the water on the lens.

Tonight we're staying at Lochsa Lodge outside of Powell, ID. I'm hoping my shoes, socks and gloves dry out during the night. Forecast tomorrow looks like possibly rain again.
