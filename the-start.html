The Start

I'm starting a day early since rain has been forecast for the next couple of days. I want to get a head start and not do as many miles the first two days. My plan is to ride about 60-70 miles today and stay at Lochsa Lodge in Idaho.

Stopped and got breakfast and a bag of pain au chocolat for the road. Mmmmm

All loaded up! I decided to wear a small backpack. I don't generally like to ride with one, but I wanted a little extra space for food and other things I may pick up along the way.
